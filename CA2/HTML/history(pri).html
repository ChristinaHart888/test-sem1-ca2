Primary School

  From 2011-2016, I studied in Zhangde Primary School. I do not have much of an impression of the school as back then, I enjoyed defying my teachers, being the naughty defiant kid in class. I would often cause a rukus in the middle of class by either talking back to the teacher or by not handing in my homework, forcing the teacher to waste her time, singleing out everyone in the class to find who did not hand in the assignment.

Achievments

  I had a couple of achievements back in Primary School. For instance, I got best in progress for my class in 2013, as well as a couple of medals for my school's sports day best of which was a bronze medal for relay running. But most prominent of all, I was the emcee for many important school events like award giving, graduation day, and as well as being a host for my school's inagural reunion dinner in 2015, when I was still in primary 5.

Graduation

  I graduated in 2016 with a T-score of 203 and a sigh of relief. That score may sound abyssmal to many of you, but for me, I was content with getting above 200 as I only achieved a score of about 170 during my prelims. Thus, the fact that I could get into the express stream was a miracle in and of itself in my eyes.
